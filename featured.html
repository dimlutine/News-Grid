Half-Lit Soccer

Written By: Rex Muscles
July 12, 2019
Sportsball!

Sportsball fans from all over the block have come together en masse to marvel at the most awesome version of soccer to date.

The old sport gets a fresh new coat of blood as members of two teams try to put a little sphere into a net with only one spotlight to illuminate the field.

34 deaths and one injury have been reported in the 15 minutes that this sport has been a thing. Fans and producers are hopeful that these numbers will increase drastically.
